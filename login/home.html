Meet the Team

Who We Are

We are five Brooklyn College undergraduate CIS students who had a very difficult time understanding our major requirements, choosing a career path, and finding resources to improve our tech skills so we came up with this interactive and user-friendly website to have everything in one platform.

Mission

Our main goal is to have anyone interested in CIS degree to understand the type of major they should choose in depth. We have included three popular CIS major requirements, various career paths to pursue, and resources to be successful in their field.
